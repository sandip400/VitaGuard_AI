⚙️ Risk Calculation Formula

Each lifestyle factor is assigned a weight. Your total weighted score is normalized to a 0–10 scale.

Factor Condition Weight
BMI BMI > 30 (Obese) +2.0
BMI BMI 27–30 (Overweight) +1.5
Sleep < 6 hours/night +1.5
Exercise < 2 days/week +1.5
Family History Multiple conditions +2.0
Family History Any single condition +1.5
Stress > 7 / 10 +1.0
Screen Time > 8 hours/day +1.0
Diet Fast food / irregular +1.0

Formula: Final Score = (Total Points / Max Possible Points) × 10
